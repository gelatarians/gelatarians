Terms of Service

All owners of gelatarians are covered under the following Terms of Service. By becoming the owner of a gelatarian or its subspecies, you agree to these terms.

General rules

  1. Some of our rules may apply both inside and outside of our community (bigotry, supporting criminal activity, theft, etc.) , meaning even if you are not breaking the rules directly in our community/server but do outside on your other socials, you may still be banned depending on the offense.
  2. Any and all forms of bigotry are strictly forbidden and will result in a ban.
  3. Anyone found to be participating in illegal activity or enabling/supporting said activities (pedophilia, zoophilia, grooming/abuse, incest/proship, etc) will be banned.
  4. The Gelatarian discord server is a 15+ space. Users found to be under 15 will be removed from the discord until they turn the minimum age.
  5. Staff members must be treated with patience and politeness just like any other member. Any aggression towards members/staff will result in warnings/bans.
  6. Attempting to find loopholes in our rules will result in a warning and may result in a ban depending on the severity/frequence.
  7. NSFW of any kind is stricly forbidden within our species. While you are free to create NSFW content of your gelatarian, we ask that it not be shared within our communities and that it be properly hidden from minors on your socials.
  8. Theft of any kind (tracing, claiming other's art/designs as yours, AI "art", scamming, etc.) will result in a ban. In addition to this, please always credit artists when sharing art, designs, bases, etc within our community.
  9. While swearing is allowed in our community, we do not allow members to use slurs, even if they can be reclaimed.

Ownership

  1. Gelatarians and all of their subspecies are closed species, meaning you may not create one without a MYO slot.
  2. All designs are for non-commercial purposes only.
  3. The Gelatarian team is not trying to gatekeep or claim any of our species' traits as our own. You are free to create ocs inspired by our species, but they will NOT be official and may not be used within the species' events/activities until a MYO slot is used.
  4. The original designer should always be credited for the design.
  5. If a gelatarian is found to be a stolen/copied design, they will be permanently voided and the theif will be warned/banned. If you obtained a gelatarian from another user and later find out they are a stolen/ripped off design, please open a report and provide our staff team with proof so we can compensate you.

Ownership Transfers

  1. To transfer to a user, you must fill out the transfer form on this forum post and submit it in the comments. It will be transferred by an admin asap, please stay patient!
  2. Upon trading, reselling or gifting the design to another individual, the previous owner agrees to transfer all ownership rights to the recipient.
  3. Gelatarians may only be traded between users if they are official (i.e. you may not advertize a character as a gelatarian if they do not have a masterlist entry). While you are allowed to gift/raffle away unofficial designs within our community, you are not allowed to put them for art, character or monetary offers.
  4. Staff/members may never revoke a gelatarian from a user no matter the reason.

Co ownership

  1. While co-owning is allowed within this species, we highly discourage creating 2 toyhouse profiles for the same gelatarian. Only 1 profile may be linked to the masterlist.
  2. All owners must be listed on the masterlist. There MUST be a main owner; usually the one who holds the toyhouse profile and who will have the gelatarian's masterlist entry visible on their site profile.
  3. If a co-owner was added after the oc was submitted to the masterlist, you must send in a report to have the owner list updated.
  4. To remove a co-owner, you must provide proof that you have permission to remove their co-own status. Exceptions may apply (ex: blacklisted users will have all co-own mentions removed from the masterlist), please contact a staff member if unsure what to do.
  5. If trading a co-owned gelatarian, you must provide proof that every owner has agreed to put the character up for offer.
  6. In cases of ownership disputes between co-owners that are brought into our community, staff has the right to void the gelatarian. We are not responsible in any way for ownership disputes, and those must be resolved privately between owners.

MYO designs

MYO's can be obtained through the following means:

  • MYO sales (USD via our usd shop or species currency via the species' shop)
  • MYO Giveaways
  • Event participation
  • Trades between users

After you've obtained your MYO, the slot will be kept up with on the masterlist.

MYO Designs and Approval

  1. Please follow the respective species design guides to make sure you create a design that 100% reflects the species.
  2. While we greatly encourage creativity, you must still at the minimum follow the mandatory traits needed for us to approve your design.
  3. Designs may:
    • Be similar in concept or themes to other designs as long as the final design be visually distinct from any existing designs.
    • Be based on other designs that you own as long as the original designer is aware and gives permission for this. Trades of any MYO designs derived from other designs must be traded with its original design.
  4. To submit your design, please visit this forum thread and follow the directions.

Character based characters / CBCs

  1. You must ALWAYS disclose when a gelatarian you are submitting is a CBC. Failure to do so will result in a gentle warning, but may turn into a void/ban if you are found to be doing it maliciously.
  2. If you fail to disclose that your gelatarian is a CBC and it's source material breaks our rules, the character will be voided with no chance to unvoid.
  3. CBCs may not be identical copies of their source character/media, and must still clearly be their own character.
  4. Characters based on songs, albums, game locations, consoles, IP-based species, etc still fall under the CBC umbrella and must be disclosed when submitted.
  5. We do not allow characters based on real people; this includes celebrities, content creators, singers, historical figures, etc.
  6. Media of pornographic, criminal or generally problematic/harmful/highly disturbing nature are not allowed within our species. There is not fixed list of medias banned from being turned into CBCs as it is subject to vary from character to character.

Edits and Redesigns

  1. All edits must be based on the original design and be recognizably the same design.
  2. Owners may choose to revert designs to any already approved redesign if desired, even if the old design has traits the the new one doesn't, since it was official at some point you are still able to revert back to it for free if you wish without providing any items.
  3. If reverting back to an older design, it is your responsibility to provide proof that the design was official at some point in time. Staff will not go searching for it, and we will reject any revert submissions if you fail to provide proof.
  4. Small design changes such as minor hue/color shifts, outfit/accessory/hairstyle changes, etc do not need any redesign items to be approved.
  5. Removing a trait from an existing gelatarian is free, but will not refund you any items.
  6. Adding a trait to an existing gelatarian will require the item corresponding to the trait you wish to add.
  7. There are no cooldowns on redesigns, and there is no real limit to how many redesigns a gelatarian can go through, tho it must ALWAYS still resemble the original design.
  8. If redesigning a gelatarian to turn them into a CBC, you must have the designer's permission AND state the CBC clearly in your redesign request.
  9. If redesigning a CBC gelatarian to become a regular gelatarian (removing all aspects tied to the IP/media they are based on), please state so in your request so the CBC note can be removed.
  10. All redesigns must be sumitted to the masterlist to be official/used within our species.

Trading / Reselling

  1. Users are not allowed to create gelatarian adoptables unless they are a guest artist.
  2. Gelatarians do not have a trading cooldown, tho it is highly discouraged to get gelatarians exclusively to retrade or resell them. Mod action may be taken if extreme fodder is happening.
  3. Gelatarians MUST have an up to date value log to be resold for real currency.
  4. Gelatarians may not be sold for more than their current value log.
  5. Commissioned art can be added to a gelatarian's worth via appraisal, but we ask you provide proof of the commission price.
  6. Personal art and gift/trade art do not count towards a gelatarian's value log to prevent users from boosting worth with false/exaggerated art prices.
  7. Gelatarians can be traded for art, characters, customs, gelatarian items/currency, etc with no limitations.
  8. Gelatarian value logs are listed in USD, please make sure to specify which currency the added worth is when trying to submit a worth change.
  9. In cases of IRL emergency, you may contact a staff member to have permission to resell a gelatarian for more than the value log. Please provide information on your situation, and we will give you a temporary appraisal. Abusing this rule by faking emergencies (i.e. clearly using the money for purposes unrelated to your supposed emergency) will result in a ban. In cases of doubt, staff may verify your activity outside the species.

Currency / Items

  1. Gelatarian currency has no real currency worth and CANNOT be sold for real currency.
  2. Gelatarian items may only be sold for real currency if they were originally bought via the USD shop, meaning any item bought with gelatarian currency cannot be sold for real currency afterwards. Staff will ask you for proof of purchase if you are trying to resell a gelatarian item for real currency.
  3. Gelatarian currency can be traded between users, and you are allowed to sell art, designs, ocs, customs etc for gelatarian currency
  4. If offering custom gelatarian designs to other users, make sure to specify that they will NOT be official until the proper items are used.
  5. Gelatarian items and currency can be traded between users.
  6. Taking on commissions for our species' currency is allowed.

Voiding / Unvoiding

  1. Voided gelatarians cannot be used within the species. They are considered one-off designs and no longer apart of our species until unvoided.
  2. Banned/blacklisted users' gelatarians will all be voided after the ban goes through.
  3. Voided gelatarians can be unvoided at any time.
  4. If you are found to be voiding and unvoiding gelatarians to bypass our other rules (reselling, redesigning, etc) , you will be banned. If we see you actively voiding and selling every gelatarian you get while continuing to get more, for example, we will take action.
  5. If a gelatarian was redesigned while voided, you must provide the corresponding items to unvoid them with their new design. Any changes made to a gelatarian while they were voided is unofficial and therefore cannot be approved within our species until unvoided and properly redesigned.

Refunds

  1. All monetary purchases are final and may not be refunded. This includes item purchases, mod customs/adopts, etc. There will be no exceptions to this rule, regardless of your situation. Please make sure you can afford to make purchases within our species before buying anything with real currency.
  2. If purchased with gelatarian currency, items can be refunded if you provide proof you bought them yourself. We will not refund items you obtained through trades with other users.
  3. If banned from our community due to breach of TOS, you will not be refunded or compensated for your lost items.

  • They are no longer allowed to own any gelatarians, and any they obtain after the blacklist will be voided.
